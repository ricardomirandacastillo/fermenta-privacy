Fermenta – Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Fermenta ("we", "our", or "us") is an artisan baking assistant app for iOS and Android. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and your rights over it.

1. Who We Are

Fermenta is developed and operated by Miranda R. For questions or data requests, contact us at ric.ignaciom@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

Account identifiers — Anonymous Firebase UID (always); email address and display name (only if you create a full account or sign in with Google). Used for authentication and account management.

Profile data — Baker name, skill level, baking intent, starter name. Used to personalize the app experience.

User-generated content — Starters (name, feeding schedule, flour ratios), bake logs (formula, timeline, notes), recipe variants, community post captions (up to 280 characters). Used for core app functionality.

Photos — Photos you select from your camera roll for community posts (publicly visible to other users) and for starter health analysis (private, only visible to you). Used for community sharing and AI-powered starter analysis.

App activity — AI request count (per month), feeding timestamps, bake timestamps, likes on community posts. Used for quota enforcement, notifications, and community features.

Approximate location — When you tap the "auto-detect temperature" button, your device's IP address is sent to ipapi.co to estimate your city-level location, which is then used to fetch the outdoor temperature for fermentation timing. This only happens when you tap that button. You can skip it and enter temperature manually.

Subscription status — A boolean flag (premium: yes/no). No payment card data or transaction IDs are ever stored by Fermenta. Purchases are handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play.

We do not collect precise GPS location, contacts, call logs, clipboard content, or any sensor data beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide core app features: calculator, timeline, starter tracker, recipe vault
  • Enable community features: publishing variants, feed, likes
  • Power AI-assisted recipe suggestions via our server-side proxy (your identity is never sent to the AI provider)
  • Send local notifications for starter feeding reminders and bake stage alerts — processed entirely on your device, no server involved
  • Enforce usage quotas and premium entitlements
  • Delete your data when you request account deletion

4. Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database, file storage, server functions. Receives account data, user content, and photos. Privacy policy

RevenueCat — Subscription and purchase management. Receives app instance ID and subscription status; no payment details. Privacy policy

Groq — AI recipe suggestions. Receives your recipe formula (percentages and weights), your text request, and your starter name. Requests are routed through our Firebase Cloud Function — your personal identity is not attached. Privacy policy

ipapi.co — IP-based city-level location for temperature auto-detection. Receives your device's IP address only when you tap the detect button. Privacy policy

Open-Meteo — Outdoor temperature fetch. Receives approximate latitude/longitude derived from IP; no personal identifier. Terms

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data.

  • Community posts — when you publish a recipe variant to the community feed, your baker name, recipe formula, caption, and any attached photo are visible to all Fermenta users.
  • Legal obligations — we may disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

6. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained for as long as your account exists.
  • Anonymous accounts persist until you delete the app or request deletion.
  • Starter analysis photos are retained in private Firebase Storage until you delete your account or the starter.
  • Community posts remain visible after account deletion unless you delete them first.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Delete your account — Settings → Account → Delete Account. Permanently deletes your profile, starters, and bake logs.
  • Skip temperature detection — do not tap the auto-detect button. The field defaults to 20°C and accepts manual input.
  • Opt out of notifications — Settings → Notifications, or your device's system settings.
  • Data access or portability requests — email ric.ignaciom@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.
  • EEA/UK users — you have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, and objection.
  • California residents — under CCPA you have the right to know what personal information we collect and to request deletion. We do not sell personal information.

8. Children

Fermenta is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS.
  • Data is stored in Firebase, encrypted at rest by Google.
  • API keys and secrets live in server environment variables — never in the app binary.
  • Premium entitlement and AI quota are enforced server-side, not by the client.

10. Changes to This Policy

When we update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. For material changes, we will notify you via an in-app notice.
